About Me

Hi, Everyone! My name is Edward Magdaraog. I took Bachelor of Science in Information Technology at Technological Institute of the Philippines, Quezon City. Specializing in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI). I began my journey in designing during college, where I discovered my passion for designing. When the pandemic began, I took the opportunity to develop my skills, working online as a graphic designer. Developing differerent logos for my clients, and I do business in t-shirt printing, mugs, stickers, and decal creation. My field and my enthusiasm drove me to do it. These experiences taught me the value of creativity, client collaboration, and meeting deadlines.

After graduating in September 2024, I took the initiative to deepen my expertise in UI/UX design. I have been self-studying, mastering essential tools in Figma, and expanding my skills in web development—culminating in this web portfolio to showcase my capabilities. To further enhance my knowledge, I recently completed the Google UX Design course, solidifying my understanding of user-centered design principles. As a UI/UX designer, I specialize in crafting intuitive, accessible, and visually compelling experiences. I am proficient in core design principles such as typography, color contrast, repetition, alignment, and hierarchy—ensuring that every design is both aesthetically pleasing and functionally effective. My approach focuses on translating ideas into seamless user experiences that prioritize usability and engagement. To stay ahead of industry trends, I actively engage in continuous learning through design communities and industry blogs, ensuring my work remains innovative and aligned with best practices. I am excited to collaborate with professionals and contribute to projects that push the boundaries of design excellence.

Outside of work, I enjoy spending time with loved ones, grabbing coffee with friends, playing games, listening to music, or simply relaxing at home while watching movies.
