About Me

I started college back in 2015, and everything was fine until the pandemic hit. When the pandemic began, I stopped for over two years, even though I only had my OJT and capstone left to accomplish.

During the pandemic, I developed my skills as a graphic designer, developing different logos for my clients, and I do business in t-shirt printing, stickers, and decals. My field and my enthusiasm drove me to do it. Sometimes, during my free time, I used to code for practices to become a professional programmer. Until then I realized that I am more passionate about designing a web and mobile applications. Even during college, my role in group projects were constantly focused on designing as front-end developer.

Then, I resumed my studies in September 2022, starting immediately with my internship and programming was required. So then, I chose to become an app developer. Luckily, I finished it within three months. Afterwards, I started my capstone project right after January 2023, and our group accomplished it in July 2023. I was relieved that I finally completed everything.

Until conflict came, there was only one week left to prepare for completing the requirements on our upcoming graduation ceremony. While waiting for my graduation this coming September 2024, I started self-studying at the beginning of this year because I didn't want to be idle.
